Llamas vs Alpacas....

What's the real difference anyway?

Llama facts

Llamas
  • Llamas have coarse hair
  • Llamas are independent creatures
  • Llamas have a long face
  • Llamas have long, curvy, banana-shaped ears

To learn more about llamas, visit Wikipedia

Alpacas
  • Alpacas have a soft, fine coat of hair
  • Alpacas are herd-like animals
  • Alpacas have a shorter, smooshed face
  • Alpacas have short, pointy ears

To learn more about alpacas, visit Wikipedia
